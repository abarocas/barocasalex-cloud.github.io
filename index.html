Who We Are

About Our Club

Small Town Donations started with a simple idea: what if kids could decide where charity money goes? We collect donated items from our community, sell them on eBay, and then let kids vote on which charity gets the money each month.

We believe that even small towns can make a big difference. Every toy, book, or gadget someone doesn't need anymore can become a donation that changes lives. And by letting kids vote, we're teaching the next generation that their voice matters.

Our club is run by three awesome founders who care about making the world a little better, one donation at a time.
